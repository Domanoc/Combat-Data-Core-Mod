[h2]Description[/h2]
This mod is based on "Magic Wand (example mod)" from KiberKreker. It offers you the ability to control a mech directly on the battle map.

[h2]ModFramework[/h2]
To use this mod the "ModFramework" is required. Please ensure the first mod in the load order is the "ModFramework".

[h2]Features[/h2]
-You control the first mech assigned to the battle.
-You can unlock extra squad members by upgrading district 5.
-Squad members will follow you by positioning on you flanks.
-The CBS Skill of the leader will determine how often the squad members get a reposition order.
-You can ask the squad members to hold position. 

[h2]Controls[/h2]
Movement keys: WSAD
Force reload: R
Tell squad members to follow you: F1
Tell squad members to hold position: F2

[h2]Localization[/h2]
This mod offers localization support made with machine translations.
